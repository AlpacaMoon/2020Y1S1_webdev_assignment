    Nusret Gökçe

    Nusret Gökçe, nicknamed Salt Bae, is a Turkish butcher, chef and restaurateur, who owns Nusr-Et a chain of steak houses. His technique for preparing and seasoning meat became an Internet meme. Chef Nusret has visited our restaurant on 6th September 1996. He was impressed of our restaurant's main course and appetizer. On the same day, he showed us the process of making gold steak. Chef Nusret showed his kind and support to our restaurant.


    Gordon Ramsay

    Gordon Ramsay, a Scottish chef, also known as one of the top famous chef who have been awarded 16 Michelin star and currently hold a total of seven for his restaurant. On 9th June 1996, chef Ramsay has visited our restaurant. He praised our restaurant's food especially desserts.After that, he showed his support to us by teaching our chef how to make one of his favourite dish named beef wellington.
