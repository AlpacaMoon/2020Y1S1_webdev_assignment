    OUR HISTORY


    Our organization's name is called L’ovest 69 Restaurant. L’ovest 69 Restaurant began on 6th September, 1969 in the city of Rome, Italy. As a sign of multiculturalism, we serve a wide variety of western food including American, British, Mexican, Italian, Spanish, German, and much more! Do not worry about the taste of it as we have a specified chef for each country's menu, each highly-trained and working in a highly-maintained environment. We are always improving ourselves and wish all of our customers could enjoy their session.
